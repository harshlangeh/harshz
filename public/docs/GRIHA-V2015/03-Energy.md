## Criterion 3.1 — Energy Efficiency (13 pts)

### Intent
To achieve significant reduction in building energy consumption through a combination of passive design and efficient active systems vs. a baseline reference building.

### Points Scale (% Improvement over ECBC / NBC Baseline)

| Energy Improvement | Points |
|-------------------|--------|
| 10% | 1 |
| 15% | 3 |
| 20% | 5 |
| 25% | 7 |
| 30% | 9 |
| 35% | 11 |
| 40% or more | 13 |

### Passive Design Strategies

**Envelope:**
- Window-to-wall ratio (WWR): recommended 20–40% for most climates
- Glazing: Low-e coatings with SHGC ≤ 0.25, U-value ≤ 2.0 W/m²K
- Walls: insulated cavity walls or 230 mm brick + 50 mm EPS/XPS
- Roof: RCC + 75 mm EPS/XPS + screed, or inverted roof with gravel

**Orientation and Shading:**
- Building axis along East-West reduces solar exposure on long facades
- Chajjas (horizontal overhangs) on South facade: projection = 0.6 × storey height
- Vertical fins on East and West facades

### Active System Efficiency

| System | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Chiller (>500 TR) | COP ≥ 5.5 |
| Packaged AC | ≥ 5-star BEE |
| Interior lighting | ≤ 5 W/m² (office) |
| Pumps | IE3 motors |
| Lifts | Regenerative drives |

**Energy Simulation:** EnergyPlus, eQUEST, DesignBuilder, IES-VE accepted.

### Technologies
- **Solar PV:** Most common; 1 kWp ≈ 1400–1600 units/year
- **Solar thermal:** For hot water; replaces electric geysers
- **Wind:** Small wind turbines (if site conditions allow)
- **Biogas:** From organic waste on-site

## Criterion 3.3 — Zero ODP Materials (Mandatory, 0 pts)

All refrigerants and foam insulation must have ODP = 0. R-22 (CFC/HCFC) is prohibited. This is a gate requirement — the project cannot be rated without compliance.

Acceptable refrigerants: R-410A (ODP=0), R-32 (ODP=0), R-717 Ammonia (ODP=0), R-290 Propane (ODP=0).
Acceptable insulation: EPS (CO₂-blown), XPS (CO₂-blown), mineral wool, glass wool.
