# Section 4 — Occupant Comfort and Well Being

**Rating System:** GRIHA V2015
**Maximum Points:** 12

| Criterion | Points |

---

## Criterion 4.1 — Indoor Comfort (Visual / Thermal / Acoustic) (6 pts)

### Visual Comfort (2 pts)
- Daylight factor ≥ 2% in ≥ 50% of occupied floor area: 1 pt
- Daylight factor ≥ 2% in ≥ 75% of occupied floor area: 2 pts (replaces above)
- Simulated using DIALux, Radiance, or equivalent tools

### Thermal Comfort (2 pts)
- HVAC design meets ASHRAE 55 / NBC comfort conditions (22–26°C, 40–60% RH): 1 pt
- Thermal comfort analysis (PMV/PPD) conducted and HVAC zoned accordingly: 2 pts (replaces above)

### Acoustic Comfort (2 pts)
- All occupied spaces meet NBC Part 8 background noise targets: 1 pt
- Acoustic consultant engaged; detailed analysis submitted: 2 pts (replaces above)

**Noise targets:** Private office ≤ NC 35, Conference ≤ NC 30, Open office ≤ NC 40.

---

## Criterion 4.2 — Maintaining Good IAQ (4 pts)

### Intent
Ensure adequate fresh air ventilation and control of indoor air pollutants.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Fresh air ventilation rate ≥ 10 L/s/person (ASHRAE 62.1 / NBC) in all occupied spaces | 2 |
| CO₂ monitoring in densely occupied spaces (>25 persons) | 1 |
| No-smoking policy across entire building | 1 |
| **Total** | **4** |

CO₂ threshold: 1000 ppm warning, 1100 ppm trigger for increased ventilation.

---

## Criterion 4.3 — Low-VOC Paints and Compounds (2 pts)

### VOC Limits

| Product | Max VOC |
|---------|---------|
| Flat/matte interior paint | ≤ 50 g/L |
| Non-flat interior paint | ≤ 150 g/L |
| Adhesives (general) | ≤ 50 g/L |
| Sealants | ≤ 250 g/L |

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Low-VOC paints used in all interior spaces | 1 |
| Low-VOC adhesives, sealants, and carpets used throughout | 1 |
| **Total** | **2** |

Common low-VOC brands in India: Asian Paints EcoClean, Berger Easy Clean, Dulux Pure Brilliant White.
