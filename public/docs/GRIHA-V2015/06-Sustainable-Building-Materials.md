# Section 6 — Sustainable Building Materials

**Rating System:** GRIHA V2015
**Maximum Points:** 14

---

## Criteria Summary

| Criterion | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| 6.1 — Utilization of BIS recommended waste materials in building structure | 6 |
| 6.2 — Reduction in embodied energy of building structure | 4 |
| 6.3 — Use of low-environmental impact materials in building interiors | 4 |
| **Total** | **14** |

---

## Criterion 6.1 — BIS-Recommended Waste Materials in Structure (6 pts)

### Intent
To use industrial by-products endorsed by the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) in the structural and masonry elements of the building.

### BIS-Approved Industrial By-products

| Material | BIS Standard | Replaces |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Fly Ash** (in cement/concrete) | IS 3812 | Ordinary Portland Cement (OPC) |
| **GGBS (Ground Granulated Blast Furnace Slag)** | IS 16714 | OPC |
| **Fly Ash Bricks** | IS 12894 | Clay fired bricks |
| **Bottom Ash / Pond Ash** | IS 3812 Part 2 | Fine aggregate |
| **Recycled Aggregate** | IS 383 | Natural stone aggregate |

### Points Scale (% of materials by cost using BIS-approved waste materials)

| % BIS Waste Materials | Points |
|----------------------|--------|
| ≥ 10% | 1 |
| ≥ 20% | 2 |
| ≥ 30% | 3 |
| ≥ 40% | 4 |
| ≥ 50% | 5 |
| ≥ 60% | 6 |

## Criterion 6.2 — Reduction in Embodied Energy of Building Structure (4 pts)

### Intent
To reduce the total energy consumed in producing, transporting, and assembling the structural and masonry components.

### Embodied Energy Benchmarks

| Material | Embodied Energy (MJ/kg) |
|---------|------------------------|
| OPC Concrete (M25) | 1.2 – 1.5 |
| PPC Concrete (30% fly ash) | 0.9 – 1.1 |
| Clay Brick | 2.0 – 3.5 |
| Fly Ash Brick | 0.8 – 1.2 |
| AAC Block | 3.0 – 4.0 (lighter, so less weight total) |
| Steel (virgin) | 25 – 35 |
| Steel (recycled, EAF) | 8 – 10 |
| Timber (sustainably sourced) | 0.5 – 1.0 |

### Points Scale (% Reduction in Embodied Energy vs. Conventional Structure)

| % Reduction | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| ≥ 10% | 1 |
| ≥ 20% | 2 |
| ≥ 30% | 3 |
| ≥ 40% | 4 |

Tools for calculation: OpenLCA, Inventory of Carbon and Energy (ICE Database, Bath UK), Indian embodied energy databases.

---

## Criterion 6.3 — Low-Environmental Impact Materials in Interiors (4 pts)

### Intent
To reduce the indoor air quality impact and environmental footprint of interior finishes, furniture, and fittings.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Low-VOC paints throughout all interior spaces | 1 |
| Low-VOC adhesives, sealants, and coatings | 1 |
| Sustainably sourced or recycled timber for flooring, furniture, or panelling (FSC/PEFC certified or recycled) | 1 |
| Low-environmental-impact flooring (bamboo, cork, recycled tiles, low-VOC carpet) | 1 |
| **Total** | **4** |

**Note:** VOC criteria here overlap with Section 4.3. Projects score in both sections if they comply — the intent is reinforced across different sections.
