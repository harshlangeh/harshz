## Criterion 9.1 — Smart Metering and Monitoring (8 pts)

### Intent
To install a granular, real-time monitoring system for energy and water that enables the building operator to track actual performance vs. design targets.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Electricity sub-metering by floor or major end-use (HVAC, lighting, plug loads) | 3 |
| Water sub-metering by end-use (domestic, HVAC, irrigation, STP) | 2 |
| Energy and water dashboard accessible to facility management | 2 |
| Data logged and monthly reports generated for at least 1 year | 1 |
| **Total** | **8** |

### Metering Architecture

**Electricity Sub-meters:**
- Main LT panel → HVAC (chiller, AHU, pumps) → Lighting → Power/plug loads → Renewable energy

**Water Sub-meters:**
- Municipal main → Domestic/potable → HVAC cooling tower → Landscape/irrigation → STP

### Energy Benchmarks to Monitor Against

| Parameter | Good Performance (Office) |
|-----------|--------------------------|
| Annual EUI | ≤ 120 kWh/m²/year |
| HVAC energy fraction | ≤ 55% of total |
| Lighting energy fraction | ≤ 15% of total |
| Daily water consumption | ≤ 45 L/person |

## Criterion 9.2 — Operation, Maintenance Protocols (0 pts, Mandatory)

An O&M Manual must be prepared and handed to building owner before final GRIHA rating is awarded. Without this, the project cannot receive its star rating.

**Manual must include:**
- Descriptions of all installed green systems
- Preventive maintenance schedules (HVAC, fire, solar, STP, rainwater, BMS)
- Troubleshooting guides
- Emergency contacts and procedures
- Annual energy and water targets

---

## Criterion 9.3 — Performance Assessment for Final Rating (0 pts, Mandatory)

After 12 months of building operation, a post-occupancy performance assessment must be submitted to GRIHA. This compares actual measured performance vs. the design intent.

**Data Required for Final Rating:**
- 12 months of energy consumption data (meter readings or BMS export)
- 12 months of water consumption data
- Post-occupancy survey results (thermal comfort, IAQ, occupant satisfaction)
- Commissioning and O&M compliance confirmation

**Note:** The provisional GRIHA V2015 rating is issued at design stage. The final star rating is confirmed only after post-occupancy assessment. Actual performance can change the rating up or down.
