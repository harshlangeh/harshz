## Criterion 8.1 — Labour Safety and Sanitation (1 pt)

Comply with the Building and Other Construction Workers Act, 1996 (BOCW). Provide:
- Clean drinking water accessible to all workers
- Separate male and female toilets (1 per 25 workers)
- First aid box with trained first-aider present
- PPE for all workers (helmet, boots, gloves, harness for height work)
- Documented site safety induction before any worker starts

**Documentation:** Site safety plan, photographs, worker registration records.

---

## Criterion 8.2 — Design for Universal Accessibility (2 pts)

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Accessible ramp at main entrance (slope ≤ 1:12), accessible toilet on each floor | 1 |
| Full compliance with Harmonised Guidelines for Persons with Disabilities 2021 | 2 |
| **Total** | **2** |

Key requirements: Doorways ≥ 900 mm, corridors ≥ 1500 mm, accessible parking ≥ 4800 mm wide, lift min 1100 × 1400 mm, tactile paving at hazardous locations.

## Criterion 8.4 — Increase in Environmental Awareness (1 pt)

1 pt for at least one of:
- Green building interpretive signage installed throughout the building (explaining solar panels, STP, rainwater system, etc.)
- Annual green awareness programme for all building occupants (workshops, competitions, campaigns)
- School/college visits to the green building for educational purposes (at least 2 per year)
- Online educational content about the building's green features published and maintained

**Documentation:** Photographs of signage, programme attendance records, school visit logs.
