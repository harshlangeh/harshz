## Criterion 7.1 — Avoided Post-Construction Landfill (4 pts)

### Intent
To divert construction and demolition (C&D) waste from landfill through reuse, recycling, and other recovery methods.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| C&D waste diversion rate ≥ 25% from landfill | 1 |
| C&D waste diversion rate ≥ 50% from landfill | 2 |
| C&D waste diversion rate ≥ 75% from landfill | 3 |
| C&D waste diversion rate ≥ 90% from landfill | 4 |

### C&D Waste Diversion Strategies

| Waste Stream | Diversion Method |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Concrete rubble | Crush to recycled aggregate; use as sub-base or fill |
| Brick rubble | Clean fill, granular drainage, or decorative gravel |
| Steel reinforcement offcuts | Return to steel scrap dealer |
| Timber formwork | Reuse in subsequent structures or sell |
| Excavated soil | Landscaping fill, earthwork bunds |
| Packaging (polythene, cardboard) | Separate and recycle |

**Documentation:** Weight-based waste tracking from site inception to completion, receipts from recyclers.

---

## Criterion 7.2 — Treat Organic Waste On Site (2 pts)

### Intent
To handle the organic fraction of operational waste (food scraps, garden trimmings) on-site, diverting it from landfill and creating useful compost or energy.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| On-site organic waste treatment handles ≥ 50% of organic waste generated | 1 |
| On-site treatment handles 100% of organic waste generated | 2 |

### Technologies

| Technology | Best For | Output |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| **Aerobic composting** | Buildings with green space | Compost |
| **Vermicomposting** | Medium-scale buildings | High-quality compost |
| **Biogas digester** | Buildings with canteen | Biogas + fertilizer slurry |
| **OWC (Organic Waste Converter)** | Space-constrained urban buildings | Compost (fast processing) |

### Sizing Guide
- 200-person office with canteen: ~40 kg organic waste/day
- Choose equipment with ≥ 40 kg/day throughput
- Compost output: approximately 10 kg/day from 40 kg input (after moisture reduction)
