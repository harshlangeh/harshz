## Criterion 2.1 — Air and Water Pollution Control (1 pt)

Implement dust suppression (water spraying, covered trucks, wind barriers) and prevent sediment-laden runoff from leaving the site (silt fences, check dams, sediment basins).

1 pt for a documented dust and stormwater pollution prevention plan, with site photographs as evidence.

---

## Criterion 2.2 — Preserve and Protect Landscape During Construction (4 pts)

### Intent
To minimise damage to existing trees, water bodies, and vegetation during the construction period.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Tree protection plan: fencing and buffer zones around all trees to be retained | 1 |
| Topsoil stripped (top 150–300 mm), stockpiled (≤ 2 m height), and reused post-construction | 2 |
| Water bodies and drainage channels on/adjacent to site protected from siltation and contamination | 1 |
| **Total** | **4** |

**Tree Protection Zone (TPZ):** minimum radius = 12 × trunk diameter from tree centre. No machinery, storage, or compaction within TPZ.

---

## Criterion 2.3 — Construction Management Practices (4 pts)

### Intent
To manage the entire construction process systematically to minimise waste, pollution, noise, and community disruption.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Documented Construction Management Plan (CMP) submitted before work begins | 1 |
| C&D waste: ≥ 25% diverted from landfill | 1 |
| C&D waste: ≥ 50% diverted from landfill | 2 (replaces above) |
| Noise management: noisy operations limited to permitted hours; community notified | 1 |
| **Total** | **4** |

**C&D Waste Types:**
- Concrete rubble → crushed recycled aggregate
- Bricks → clean fill or crushed for sub-base
- Steel → scrap dealer
- Timber → reuse or biomass
- Packaging → segregate and recycle

**Documentation Required:** CMP, waste tracking log, weight receipts from recyclers, site photographs.
