# Section 10 — Innovation

**Rating System:** GRIHA V2015
**Maximum Points:** 4 (Bonus)

---

## Criterion 10.1 — Innovation (4 pts, Optional)

Up to 4 bonus points for strategies that exceed GRIHA V2015 criteria or address sustainability dimensions not covered in sections 1–9.

---

## What Qualifies

### Exemplary Performance
- Net-Zero Energy Building (NZEB)
- Net-Zero Water (100% self-sufficient)
- ≥ 90% C&D waste diversion
- Carbon neutral construction (verified LCA showing zero net embodied carbon)

### New Technology Not in GRIHA V2015
- Building Integrated Photovoltaics (BIPV) at meaningful scale
- Phase Change Materials (PCM) in walls, slabs, or ceilings
- Electrochromic/smart glass for dynamic shading control
- Green walls (planted facades) with measurable insulation and biodiversity benefit
- AI-based BMS with predictive energy optimisation
- Earth-air heat exchangers (underground cooling/heating tunnels)

### Social Innovation
- Public-accessible green space open 24/7
- On-site green building education centre
- Traditional or local craft-based building techniques at significant scale

---

## How to Submit

Prepare a brief (2–4 pages per innovation) covering:
1. What is novel / not captured in GRIHA V2015
2. Description and quantified benefit
3. Supporting calculations or third-party validation

Maximum 4 innovations, 1 pt each, as evaluated by GRIHA Council.
