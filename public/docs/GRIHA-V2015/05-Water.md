## Criterion 5.4 — On-site Water Reuse (5 pts)

| Sub-criterion | Points |

## Criterion 5.5 — Rainwater Recharge (2 pts)

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Recharge pits, percolation wells, or infiltration trenches provided to recharge groundwater | 1 |
| Recharge capacity designed to capture 100% of roof runoff during design storm event | 2 |
| **Total** | **2** |

**Sizing:** Runoff (L) = Rainfall (mm) × Roof Area (m²) × Runoff coefficient (0.85)

**Recharge Structures:** Borewell recharge (via filter), percolation trench (filled with gravel), recharge pit (1 m × 1 m × 2 m with gravel fill), infiltration gallery.
