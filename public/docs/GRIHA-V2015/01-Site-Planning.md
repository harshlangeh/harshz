# Section 1 — Site Planning

**Rating System:** GRIHA V2015
**Maximum Points:** 8

---

## Criteria Summary

| Criterion | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| 1.1 — Site Selection | 1 |
| 1.2 — Low-impact design | 4 |
| 1.3 — Design to mitigate UHIE | 2 |
| 1.4 — Site Imperviousness Factor | 1 |
| **Total** | **8** |

---

## Criterion 1.1 — Site Selection (1 pt)

### Intent
To avoid building on ecologically sensitive land (wetlands, forests, prime agricultural land) and to select brownfield or previously developed sites where possible.

1 pt for site not located on:
- Protected ecologically sensitive zones
- Agricultural land (Class I or II)
- Wetland or water body edge (within 50 m)
- Slopes > 25%

Brownfield redevelopment (previously developed land) earns this point automatically.

---

## Criterion 1.2 — Low-impact Design (4 pts)

### Intent
To reduce stormwater runoff and manage rainfall on-site through design strategies that limit impervious surfaces and promote infiltration.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Site imperviousness ≤ 50% of total site area (i.e., ≥ 50% permeable) | 2 |
| Stormwater management: on-site detention/retention provided for 24-hr design storm | 1 |
| Bioswales or rain gardens or vegetated swales provided along site drainage paths | 1 |
| **Total** | **4** |

**Permeable Surfaces Count:**
- Grass, planting beds, exposed soil
- Gravel/pebble paths
- Permeable paving (grass pavers, gravel-filled cells, permeable concrete)
- Open grid pavers

---

## Criterion 1.3 — Design to Mitigate UHIE (2 pts)

### Intent
To reduce the Urban Heat Island Effect by using reflective materials on roofs and hard paving surfaces.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Cool roof: SRI ≥ 78 for ≥ 50% of roof area | 1 |
| Non-roof paving: SRI ≥ 29 OR ≥ 50% of parking/paths shaded | 1 |
| **Total** | **2** |

**SRI (Solar Reflectance Index):** White roof paint has SRI ~90; bare concrete SRI ~35; dark bitumen SRI ~0.

Popular cool roof options: white/lime wash, China mosaic tile, thermoplastic polyolefin (TPO) membrane.

---

## Criterion 1.4 — Site Imperviousness Factor (1 pt)

### Intent
Complementary to 1.2 — specifically focused on the ratio of hard paved surface to total site area.

1 pt for total impervious area ≤ 40% of total site area, verified with a site area breakdown (in m²) of each surface type.

**Calculation:**
```
Imperviousness (%) = (Total hard impervious area) / (Total site area) × 100
```

Hard impervious surfaces: RCC paving, asphalt roads, concrete walkways, built rooftop area.
