## Criterion 4.1 — Visual Comfort (4 pts, Partly Mandatory)

### Intent
To ensure adequate natural daylight and appropriate artificial lighting so occupants can perform tasks comfortably without eyestrain or glare.

### Mandatory Requirement
All occupied spaces must meet minimum illumination levels as per **NBC Part 8** (National Building Code). Lighting power density (LPD) must not exceed NBC prescribed limits.

### Points Available

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Daylight factor ≥ 2% in ≥ 50% of occupied floor area | 2 |
| Daylight factor ≥ 2% in ≥ 75% of occupied floor area | 3 (replace above) |
| Glare control provided in all spaces with computer screens (e.g., blinds, diffusers) | 1 |
| **Total** | **4** |

### Daylight Factor (DF) Explained
- **DF = (Indoor illuminance / Outdoor illuminance) × 100%**
- DF of 2% means 2% of available outdoor daylight reaches the interior
- Achieved through: larger windows, skylights, light shelves, high-ceiling design

### Good Daylighting Design Tips
- Provide windows on 2+ facades for balanced light distribution
- Use light shelves to bounce light deeper into the floor plate
- Interior walls painted white or light colours improve daylight distribution
- Daylight simulation using tools: DIALux, Velux Daylight, Radiance, IES-VE

### Documentation Required
- Daylight simulation report (room-by-room DF values)
- Floor plan showing percentage of area meeting DF ≥ 2%
- Glare control device specifications

## Criterion 4.2 — Thermal & Acoustic Comfort (2 pts, Partly Mandatory)

### Intent
To ensure the building maintains comfortable temperature and humidity conditions and provides adequate noise control for occupant well-being.

### Mandatory Requirement
All air-conditioned spaces must be designed to maintain temperatures within the comfort band prescribed by NBC/ASHRAE Standard 55 (22–26°C for sedentary occupants).

### Points Available

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Thermal comfort analysis conducted using ASHRAE 55 or NBC criteria; HVAC zoning designed accordingly | 1 |
| Acoustic performance meets NBC Part 8 criteria for occupied spaces (background noise levels appropriate per space type) | 1 |
| **Total** | **2** |

### Thermal Comfort Benchmarks

| Space Type | Recommended Temperature | Relative Humidity |
|------------|------------------------|-------------------|
| Office/Work areas | 23–26°C | 40–60% |
| Meeting rooms | 22–25°C | 40–60% |
| Corridors/lobbies | 24–28°C (mixed mode acceptable) | |

### Acoustic Design Guidelines

| Space Type | Maximum Background Noise (dB) |
|------------|-------------------------------|
| Private office | NC 30–35 |
| Open office | NC 35–40 |
| Conference room | NC 25–30 |
| Classroom | NC 30–35 |
| Lobby | NC 40–45 |

### Documentation Required
- HVAC zone plan with temperature setpoints per zone
- Acoustic consultant report or calculation
- Manufacturer data for sound-attenuating products (duct lining, acoustic ceiling tiles)

## Criterion 4.3 — Maintaining Indoor Air Quality (7 pts, Optional)

### Intent
To protect occupant health by ensuring the building provides sufficient fresh air, limits indoor pollutant sources, and monitors air quality continuously.

### Points Available

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Fresh air ventilation rate ≥ 10 L/s/person (per ASHRAE 62.1 or NBC) in all occupied spaces | 2 |
| Low-VOC paints, adhesives, sealants, and coatings used throughout | 1 |
| Low-VOC carpets, flooring, and furniture (per applicable GREENGUARD or equivalent certification) | 1 |
| CO₂ monitoring in densely occupied spaces (>25 persons) with alarm/damper auto-control | 2 |
| Dedicated smoking zones (if any) fully isolated with separate exhaust | 1 |
| **Total** | **7** |

### VOC Limits — Paints and Coatings

| Product Type | Maximum VOC Content |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Flat/matte interior paint | ≤ 50 g/L |
| Non-flat interior paint | ≤ 150 g/L |
| Anti-corrosive / primer | ≤ 250 g/L |
| Sealants (architectural) | ≤ 250 g/L |
| Adhesives (general) | ≤ 50 g/L |

### Common Low-VOC Products Available in India
- Asian Paints EcoClean (Zero VOC)
- Berger Easy Clean (Low VOC)
- Dulux Weathershield (Low VOC exterior)
- Fevicol SH (Low formaldehyde option)

### CO₂ Monitoring
- Sensor location: 1.0–1.5 m above floor in the occupied zone
- Threshold for demand-controlled ventilation (DCV): 1000 ppm (alarm), 1100 ppm (increase airflow)
- System must log data for at least 1 year post-occupancy

### Documentation Required
- HVAC specifications with fresh air rates per space and per occupant
- VOC test reports or manufacturer certificates for all paints, coatings, adhesives
- CO₂ sensor specifications and locations on floor plans
- Commissioning report confirming ventilation rates as installed
