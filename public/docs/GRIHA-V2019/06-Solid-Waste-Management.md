# Section 6 — Solid Waste Management

**Rating System:** GRIHA V2019
**Maximum Points:** 6

---

## Criteria Summary

| Criterion | Points | Compliance |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| 6.1 — Waste Management – Post Occupancy | 4 | Partly Mandatory |
| 6.2 — Organic Waste Treatment | 2 | Optional |

---

## Criterion 6.1 — Waste Management – Post Occupancy (4 pts, Partly Mandatory)

Mandatory: Waste segregation into wet/dry/hazardous per SWM Rules 2016.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Colour-coded bins at all floors (green = organic, blue = dry, red = hazardous) | 1 |
| Dedicated waste storage room with ventilation at building level | 1 |
| Formal agreement with registered recycler/aggregator | 1 |
| Monthly waste data tracked and reported by category (kg) | 1 |
| **Total** | **4** |

---

## Criterion 6.2 — Organic Waste Treatment (2 pts, Optional)

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| On-site treatment handling ≥ 50% of organic waste generated | 1 |
| On-site treatment handling 100% of organic waste generated | 2 |
| **Total** | **2** |

Technologies: Composting (aerobic), vermicomposting, biogas digester (anaerobic), in-vessel composter, OWC.

Sizing: 200-person office with canteen generates ~40 kg organic waste/day. Size equipment accordingly.
