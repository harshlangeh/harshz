## Criterion 3.1 — Energy Optimization (12 pts, Partly Mandatory)

### Mandatory Requirement
Must meet minimum energy performance per NBC Part 11 / ECBC. Project cannot be rated without compliance.

### Points Scale (% Improvement over ECBC baseline)

| Energy Savings | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| 10% improvement | 1 |
| 20% improvement | 4 |
| 30% improvement | 8 |
| 40% or more | 12 |

### Passive Design Strategies
- Building orientation: long axis East-West to minimise solar gain
- External shading: chajjas, overhangs, fins on East/West facades
- High-performance glazing: Low-e coatings, low SHGC
- Roof insulation: EPS/XPS, R-value ≥ 3.5 m²K/W
- Climate-responsive design: cross-ventilation for humid climates; thermal mass for composite/hot-dry

### Active System Efficiency
- Air conditioning: 5-star BEE rating
- Chillers: COP ≥ 5.0
- Lighting: LED throughout; LPD per NBC targets
- Pumps/fans: IE3 motors minimum

**Documentation:** Energy simulation report (EnergyPlus, eQUEST, IES-VE), equipment schedules, envelope specifications.

---

## Criterion 3.2 — Renewable Energy Utilization (5 pts, Partly Mandatory)

### Mandatory Requirement
Zero-ODP refrigerants and insulation materials (no R-22, no CFC/HCFC foam). This is also separately evaluated in Criterion 3.3.

### Points Scale (% of annual energy from on-site renewables)

| Renewable % | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| 1% | 1 |
| 5% | 2 |
| 10% | 3 |
| 15% | 4 |
| 20%+ | 5 |

### Eligible Technologies
- Solar PV (rooftop or facade)
- Solar thermal (hot water)
- Wind turbines (small-scale)
- Biogas (from on-site organic waste)
- Ground source heat pumps

**Documentation:** RE generation calculations, system design, % RE vs. annual demand.

---

## Criterion 3.3 — Low ODP and GWP Materials (1 pt, Partly Mandatory)

### Mandatory Requirement
All refrigerants must have ODP = 0. R-22 is prohibited.

### 1 Additional Point For:
Using refrigerants with GWP ≤ 150 (natural refrigerants) such as:
- R-717 (Ammonia) — GWP = 0
- R-290 (Propane) — GWP = 3
- R-744 (CO₂) — GWP = 1

**Documentation:** HVAC equipment spec sheets confirming refrigerant type + ODP/GWP values.
