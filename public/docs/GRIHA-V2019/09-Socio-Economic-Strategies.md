## Criterion 9.1 — Safety and Sanitation for Construction Workers (1 pt, Partly Mandatory)

Mandatory: Comply with BOCW Act 1996. All workers must be registered.

1 pt for implementing a documented safety and sanitation plan: PPE provision, first aid, separate male/female toilets (1:25 ratio), clean drinking water, and mandatory site induction.

---

## Criterion 9.2 — Universal Accessibility (2 pts, Optional)

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Accessible entrance, ramps, signage, accessible toilet on each floor | 1 |
| Full compliance with Harmonised Guidelines 2021 across all public areas + accessible parking | 2 |
| **Total** | **2** |

Key requirements: Ramp slope ≤ 1:12, doorways ≥ 900 mm, corridors ≥ 1500 mm, lifts min 1100 × 1400 mm, accessible parking bays ≥ 4.8 m wide.

| Staff canteen/pantry with hygienic food preparation | 1 |

---

## Criterion 9.4 — Positive Social Impact (3 pts, Partly Mandatory)

Mandatory: Project must not have displaced communities without proper rehabilitation.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Community engagement during design phase; concerns incorporated | 1 |
| CSR programme linked to building (local employment, skill development) | 1 |
| Measurable community outcome demonstrated (number of beneficiaries, environmental impact) | 1 |
| **Total** | **3** |

Examples: Local hiring, skill training for workers, green education for schools, public green space.
