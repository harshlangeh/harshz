# Section 7 — Sustainable Building Materials

**Rating System:** GRIHA V2019
**Maximum Points:** 12

---

## Criteria Summary

| Criterion | Points | Compliance |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| 7.1 — Utilization of Alternative Materials in Building | 5 | Optional |
| 7.2 — Reduction in GWP through LCA | 5 | Optional |
| 7.3 — Alternative Materials for External Site Development | 2 | Optional |

---

## Criterion 7.1 — Utilization of Alternative Materials (5 pts, Optional)

Points based on percentage of total structural and masonry materials (by cost) using recycled/alternative content:

| % Alternative Materials | Points |
|------------------------|--------|
| ≥ 10% | 1 |
| ≥ 20% | 2 |
| ≥ 30% | 3 |
| ≥ 40% | 4 |
| ≥ 50% | 5 |

**Eligible Materials:** Fly ash bricks, AAC blocks, PPC cement, GGBS concrete, recycled aggregate, bamboo (engineered), recycled steel.

---

## Criterion 7.2 — Reduction in GWP through LCA (5 pts, Optional)

LCA conducted per ISO 14040/14044. Points for % reduction in embodied carbon vs. reference building:

| GWP Reduction | Points |
|--------------|--------|
| ≥ 5% | 1 |
| ≥ 10% | 2 |
| ≥ 15% | 3 |
| ≥ 20% | 4 |
| ≥ 25% | 5 |

Tools: One Click LCA, SimaPro, OpenLCA, GaBi, Tally (Revit plugin).

---

## Criterion 7.3 — Alternative Materials for External Site Development (2 pts, Optional)

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| ≥ 25% of external paving uses permeable or alternative materials | 1 |
| ≥ 50% of external paving/site elements use alternative materials | 2 |
| **Total** | **2** |

**Examples:** Fly ash paver blocks, recycled concrete pavers, grass pavers (permeable), PICP.
