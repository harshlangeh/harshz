# Section 4 — Occupant Comfort

**Rating System:** GRIHA V2019
**Maximum Points:** 12

---

## Criteria Summary

| Criterion | Points | Compliance |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| 4.1 — Visual Comfort | 4 | Partly Mandatory |
| 4.2 — Thermal and Acoustic Comfort | 2 | Partly Mandatory |
| 4.3 — Indoor Air Quality | 6 | Partly Mandatory |

---

## Criterion 4.1 — Visual Comfort (4 pts, Partly Mandatory)

Mandatory: Meet NBC Part 8 minimum illumination levels; do not exceed LPD limits.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Daylight factor ≥ 2% in ≥ 50% of occupied floor area | 2 |
| Daylight factor ≥ 2% in ≥ 75% of occupied floor area | 3 (replaces above) |
| Glare control in all spaces with screens (blinds, diffusers) | 1 |
| **Total** | **4** |

Daylight simulation tools: DIALux, Radiance, IES-VE, VELUX Daylight Visualizer.

---

## Criterion 4.2 — Thermal and Acoustic Comfort (2 pts, Partly Mandatory)

Mandatory: All AC spaces designed for 22–26°C per ASHRAE 55 / NBC.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Thermal comfort analysis done; HVAC zones designed accordingly | 1 |
| Acoustic performance meets NBC Part 8 (background noise appropriate per space type) | 1 |
| **Total** | **2** |

**Noise targets:** Private office ≤ NC 35, Open plan ≤ NC 40, Conference room ≤ NC 30.

---

## Criterion 4.3 — Indoor Air Quality (6 pts, Partly Mandatory)

Mandatory: Fresh air ventilation must meet ASHRAE 62.1 or NBC minimum rates.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Fresh air rate ≥ 10 L/s/person in all occupied spaces | 2 |
| Low-VOC paints and coatings (≤ 50 g/L flat, ≤ 150 g/L non-flat) | 1 |
| Low-VOC flooring, carpets, furniture (GREENGUARD or equivalent) | 1 |
| CO₂ monitoring with DCV in densely occupied spaces (>25 persons) | 2 |
| **Total** | **6** |

**VOC Limits:** Flat paint ≤ 50 g/L, Non-flat ≤ 150 g/L, Sealants ≤ 250 g/L, Adhesives ≤ 50 g/L.
CO₂ threshold for demand-controlled ventilation: 1000 ppm alarm, 1100 ppm increase airflow.
