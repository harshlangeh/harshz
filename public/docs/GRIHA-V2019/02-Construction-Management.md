# Section 2 — Construction Management

**Rating System:** GRIHA V2019
**Maximum Points:** 4

---

## Criteria Summary

| Criterion | Points | Compliance |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| 2.1 — Air and Soil Pollution Control | 1 | Partly Mandatory |
| 2.2 — Topsoil Preservation | 1 | Optional |
| 2.3 — Construction Management Practices | 2 | Partly Mandatory |

---

## Criterion 2.1 — Air and Soil Pollution Control (1 pt, Partly Mandatory)

Mandatory: Comply with applicable environmental regulations; ESC plan required.

1 pt for implementing dust suppression (water spraying, wind barriers, covered trucks) AND soil erosion controls (silt fences, check dams, straw wattles).

**Documentation:** Site photographs, ESC plan, site manager logs.

---

## Criterion 2.2 — Topsoil Preservation (1 pt, Optional)

Strip and stockpile topsoil (top 150–300 mm) before excavation; stockpile must not exceed 2 m height; reuse on-site for landscaping after construction.

**Documentation:** Site photographs, landscape plan showing topsoil reinstatement, volume calculations.

---

## Criterion 2.3 — Construction Management Practices (2 pts, Partly Mandatory)

Mandatory: Basic site safety and waste management plan required.

1 pt for a documented Construction Management Plan (CMP) covering waste segregation, noise, dust, scheduling, and traffic.

1 pt for post-construction C&D waste diversion rate ≥ 50% by weight from landfill via recycling, reuse, or recovery.

**C&D Waste Diversion Options:**
- Concrete rubble → crushed aggregate
- Steel offcuts → scrap recycler
- Timber → reuse in formwork
- Packaging → separate recyclables collection

**Documentation:** CMP document, waste tracking log, weight receipts from recyclers.
