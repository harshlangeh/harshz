# Section 1 — Sustainable Site Planning

**Rating System:** GRIHA V2019
**Maximum Points:** 12

---

## Criteria Summary

| Criterion | Points | Compliance |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| 1.1 — Green Infrastructure | 5 | Partly Mandatory |
| 1.2 — Low Impact Design Strategies | 5 | Optional |
| 1.3 — Design to Mitigate UHIE | 2 | Optional |

---

## Criterion 1.1 — Green Infrastructure (5 pts, Partly Mandatory)

Mandatory: Minimum green cover per local bye-laws. Landscape plan required.

Points for green cover ≥ 20% (1 pt), ≥ 30% (2 pts), native species ≥ 50% (1 pt), permeable surfaces ≥ 30% of non-built area (1 pt).

**Documentation:** Landscape plan with species list, canopy calculations, permeable paving specs.

---

## Criterion 1.2 — Low Impact Design Strategies (5 pts, Optional)

Stormwater retention/detention for 24-hr design storm (2 pts), bioswales or rain gardens (1 pt), permeable paving for parking/pedestrian areas (1 pt), no net increase in runoff vs. pre-development (1 pt).

**Documentation:** Hydrological analysis, stormwater management plan, LID feature drawings.

---

## Criterion 1.3 — Design to Mitigate UHIE (2 pts, Optional)

Cool roof with SRI ≥ 78 for ≥ 50% of roof area (1 pt), non-roof hard surfaces SRI ≥ 29 OR ≥ 50% of parking shaded (1 pt).

**Key Definition:** SRI (Solar Reflectance Index) — white/cool surfaces have SRI 78–100; dark surfaces SRI 0–25.

**Documentation:** Roof plan with SRI values, manufacturer test reports, shading device drawings.
