# Section 10 — Performance Metering and Monitoring

**Rating System:** GRIHA V2019
**Maximum Points:** 7

---

## Criteria Summary

| Criterion | Points | Compliance |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| 10.1 — Project Commissioning | 0 | **Mandatory** |
| 10.2 — Smart Metering and Monitoring | 7 | Partly Mandatory |
| 10.3 — Operation and Maintenance Protocol | 0 | **Mandatory** |

---

## Criterion 10.1 — Project Commissioning (0 pts, Mandatory)

All HVAC and electrical systems must be commissioned and tested by the contractor before handover. Commissioning reports must be submitted. No points available — this is a gate requirement.

**Systems to commission:** HVAC (airflow, temperatures, controls), electrical (circuits, emergency power), plumbing (pressure, flow), fire protection, BMS.

---

## Criterion 10.2 — Smart Metering and Monitoring (7 pts, Partly Mandatory)

Mandatory: Main utility meters (electricity + water) must be logged. Monthly consumption reports required.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Electricity sub-metering by zone/floor or major end-use | 2 |
| Water sub-metering by end-use | 1 |
| Energy dashboard accessible to facility management (real-time data) | 2 |
| BMS integrates all systems with automated consumption alerts | 1 |
| Monthly energy/water reports benchmarked against targets | 1 |
| **Total** | **7** |

**Metering Hierarchy:**
- Electricity: Main → HVAC → Lighting → Plug loads → Solar PV generation
- Water: Municipal main → Domestic → HVAC cooling tower → Irrigation → STP

**Energy Performance Targets:**
- Office EUI ≤ 120 kWh/m²/year
- HVAC EPI ≤ 70 kWh/m²/year
- Water ≤ 45 L/person/day (non-potable)

---

## Criterion 10.3 — Operation and Maintenance Protocol (0 pts, Mandatory)

An O&M Manual must be prepared and handed to building owner/facility manager. No points — gate requirement for final rating.

**Manual must include:** System descriptions, preventive maintenance schedule, troubleshooting guide, emergency procedures, energy/water targets, green system operation guidance.

Maintenance highlights: HVAC filters monthly, chiller semi-annual, solar panel cleaning monthly, STP weekly inspection, water quality testing quarterly (IS 10500).
