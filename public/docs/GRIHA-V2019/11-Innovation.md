# Section 11 — Innovation

**Rating System:** GRIHA V2019
**Maximum Points:** 5 (Bonus)

---

## Criterion 11.1 — Innovation (5 pts, Optional)

Up to 5 bonus points for strategies that exceed GRIHA V2019 thresholds or address sustainability dimensions not in the current rating system. Each innovation earns 1–2 points at GRIHA Council's discretion.

---

## What Qualifies

### Exemplary Performance (Beyond GRIHA Thresholds)
- Net-Zero Energy Building (NZEB) — on-site generation = annual consumption
- Net-Zero Water — 100% water self-sufficiency from on-site sources
- Carbon neutral / carbon negative construction
- C&D waste diversion ≥ 90%

### New Technology Not in GRIHA V2019
- Building Integrated Photovoltaics (BIPV)
- Phase Change Materials (PCM) in walls/ceilings
- Electrochromic / smart glass
- Vertical farms / productive landscapes integrated in building
- AI-based BMS with machine learning optimisation
- Green walls (planted facades) providing measurable insulation + biodiversity benefit

### Social Innovation
- Community-accessible public green space 24/7
- Green building education centre open to public
- Local artisan/traditional craft materials used at scale

---

## Submission Process
Each innovation claim requires a 4-page brief covering:
1. What existing GRIHA V2019 criteria doesn't capture this
2. Description of the innovation
3. Quantified environmental/social benefit
4. Supporting technical drawings or case studies

Submit with design-stage documentation.
