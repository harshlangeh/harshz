# Section 5 — Water Management

**Rating System:** GRIHA V2019
**Maximum Points:** 16

---

## Criteria Summary

| Criterion | Points | Compliance |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| 5.1 — Water Demand Reduction | 4 | Partly Mandatory |
| 5.2 — Wastewater Treatment | 2 | Optional |
| 5.3 — Rainwater Management | 5 | Optional |
| 5.4 — Water Quality and Self-Sufficiency | 5 | Partly Mandatory |

---

## Criterion 5.1 — Water Demand Reduction (4 pts, Partly Mandatory)

Mandatory: All fixtures must meet minimum NBC efficiency requirements.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| WC dual flush (3/6 L), washbasin ≤ 6 L/min, shower ≤ 8 L/min | 2 |
| Native/adaptive landscaping requiring no permanent irrigation | 1 |
| On-site water recycling for flushing/irrigation | 1 |
| **Total** | **4** |

---

## Criterion 5.2 — Wastewater Treatment (2 pts, Optional)

On-site STP treating ≥ 50% of wastewater; treated water reused for flushing/irrigation: **2 pts**.

Technologies: SBR, MBBR, MBR, Extended Aeration. MBR produces highest quality output in smallest footprint.

---

## Criterion 5.3 — Rainwater Management (5 pts, Optional)

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Rainwater harvesting system captures 100% of roof runoff (24-hr design storm) | 2 |
| Storage tank sized for ≥ 10 days of non-potable demand | 1 |
| Groundwater recharge pits/percolation wells provided | 1 |
| Rainwater used for at least one potable or non-potable end use | 1 |
| **Total** | **5** |

Sizing formula: Runoff (L) = Rainfall (mm) × Roof Area (m²) × 0.85 (coefficient) × 0.85 (filter loss)

---

## Criterion 5.4 — Water Quality and Self-Sufficiency (5 pts, Partly Mandatory)

Mandatory: Drinking water must meet IS 10500:2012; NABL lab report required.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Water sub-metering for all major end uses | 1 |
| Monthly water consumption tracked by end use | 1 |
| Water self-sufficiency ≥ 30% (on-site sources) | 1 |
| Water self-sufficiency ≥ 50% | 2 |
| Water self-sufficiency ≥ 75% | 3 |
| **Total** | **5** |
