## Criterion 8.1 — Life Cycle Cost Analysis (5 pts, Optional)

### Intent
To help building owners evaluate the 25-year financial return on green investments, comparing total cost of ownership of the green design vs. a conventional baseline.

### Points Available

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| LCCA covers: CAPEX, energy cost, water cost, maintenance, replacement over 25 years | 3 |
| LCCA shows positive NPV for green measures over 25-year period | 2 |
| **Total** | **5** |

### LCCA Components

| Cost Category | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| CAPEX | Construction, equipment, green technology premium |
| Energy OPEX | Annual electricity and fuel bills |
| Water OPEX | Annual municipal water charges |
| Maintenance | Preventive maintenance costs |
| Replacement | Equipment replacement schedule (chillers, pumps, solar panels, etc.) |

### NPV Calculation
```
NPV = Σ [Annual Savings(t) / (1 + r)^t] - Initial Green Premium

Where:
- t = 1 to 25 years
- r = discount rate (8–12% for India)
- Annual Savings = Energy savings + Water savings + Maintenance reduction
```

### Typical Green Measure Paybacks (India)

| Green Measure | Payback Period |
|--------------|---------------|
| LED lighting | 2–4 years |
| Solar PV rooftop | 5–7 years |
| Solar water heating | 3–5 years |
| High-efficiency HVAC | 5–8 years |
| Rainwater harvesting | 4–7 years |

### Documentation Required
- LCCA spreadsheet with all assumptions (discount rate, escalation rates, tariffs)
- 25-year cashflow table comparing green vs. conventional
- NPV summary clearly showing whether green design is financially positive
