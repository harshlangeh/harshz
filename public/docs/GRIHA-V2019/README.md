# GRIHA V2019 — Green Rating for Integrated Habitat Assessment (2019)

> **Type:** Star Rating (1 to 5 Stars)
> **Total Points:** 100 (base) + 5 (innovation) = **105 pts**
> **Applies to:** New construction buildings in India

## Section Descriptions

### Section 1 — Sustainable Site Planning (12 pts)
Covers green infrastructure (trees, biodiversity, permeable surfaces), low impact design to manage stormwater, and urban heat island mitigation through cool roofs and reflective paving.

**Key criteria:** Green Infrastructure (5 pts, Partly Mandatory), Low Impact Design (5 pts, Optional), Design to Mitigate UHIE (2 pts, Optional)

### Section 2 — Construction Management (4 pts)
Controls site pollution (air/soil/dust), requires topsoil stripping and reuse, and demands a documented Construction Management Plan with C&D waste tracking.

**Key criteria:** Air and Soil Pollution Control (1 pt, Partly Mandatory), Topsoil Preservation (1 pt, Optional), Construction Management Practices (2 pts, Partly Mandatory)

### Section 3 — Energy Optimization (18 pts)
Largest section. Rewards passive design + efficient active systems (up to 12 pts) and on-site renewable energy generation (up to 5 pts). Also includes 1 pt for use of low-ODP and low-GWP refrigerants/materials.

**Key criteria:** Energy Optimization (12 pts, Partly Mandatory), Renewable Energy Utilization (5 pts, Partly Mandatory), Low ODP and GWP Materials (1 pt, Partly Mandatory)

### Section 4 — Occupant Comfort (12 pts)
Daylight and glare control (Visual Comfort), thermal zoning and acoustic performance (Thermal and Acoustic Comfort), and indoor air quality through ventilation rates, low-VOC materials, and CO₂ monitoring.

**Key criteria:** Visual Comfort (4 pts, Partly Mandatory), Thermal and Acoustic Comfort (2 pts, Partly Mandatory), Indoor Air Quality (6 pts, Partly Mandatory)

### Section 5 — Water Management (16 pts)
Efficient fixtures and fittings, on-site wastewater treatment and reuse, rainwater harvesting for non-potable demand, and water quality/self-sufficiency targets.

**Key criteria:** Water Demand Reduction (4 pts, Partly Mandatory), Wastewater Treatment (2 pts, Optional), Rainwater Management (5 pts, Optional), Water Quality and Self-Sufficiency (5 pts, Partly Mandatory)

### Section 6 — Solid Waste Management (6 pts)
Post-occupancy waste segregation (at least wet/dry/hazardous), dedicated waste storage with recycler agreements, and on-site organic waste treatment (composting/biogas).

**Key criteria:** Waste Management Post Occupancy (4 pts, Partly Mandatory), Organic Waste Treatment (2 pts, Optional)

### Section 7 — Sustainable Building Materials (12 pts)
Use of alternative/recycled materials in structure and interior works (fly ash, AAC, GGBS, recycled aggregate), LCA to demonstrate GWP reduction, and alternative materials for external site development.

**Key criteria:** Utilization of Alternative Materials (5 pts, Optional), Reduction in GWP through LCA (5 pts, Optional), Alternative Materials for External Site Development (2 pts, Optional)

### Section 8 — Life Cycle Costing (5 pts)
Life Cycle Cost Analysis (LCCA) demonstrating the 25-year financial case for green measures, comparing NPV of green design vs. conventional baseline.

**Key criteria:** Life Cycle Cost Analysis (5 pts, Optional)

### Section 9 — Socio-Economic Strategies (8 pts)
Worker safety on-site, universal accessibility for PwD, dedicated service staff facilities, and positive social impact programmes in the surrounding community.

**Key criteria:** Safety and Sanitation (1 pt, Partly Mandatory), Universal Accessibility (2 pts, Optional), Dedicated Facilities (2 pts, Optional), Positive Social Impact (3 pts, Partly Mandatory)

### Section 10 — Performance Metering and Monitoring (7 pts)
Smart metering for energy and water, BMS integration, and monthly performance reporting. Project commissioning and O&M Protocol are mandatory pre-conditions.

**Key criteria:** Project Commissioning (0 pts, Mandatory), Smart Metering and Monitoring (7 pts, Partly Mandatory), Operation and Maintenance Protocol (0 pts, Mandatory)

### Section 11 — Innovation (5 pts, Bonus)
Awards up to 5 bonus points for technologies or strategies exceeding GRIHA 2019 criteria or addressing sustainability dimensions not yet in the rating system.

**Key criteria:** Innovation (5 pts, Optional)

## Compliance Types

| Type | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Mandatory** | Must achieve — required for project submission |
| **Partly Mandatory** | Minimum threshold mandatory; extra points for exceeding it |
| **Optional** | Voluntary credit |
