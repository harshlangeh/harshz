# IGBC SB 2020 — Indian Green Building Council Sustainable Buildings 2020

> **Type:** Certification Level (Certified / Silver / Gold / Platinum)
> **Applies to:** New and Existing Buildings in India
> **Governing Body:** Indian Green Building Council (IGBC), a part of CII

---

## Quick Reference — Certification Thresholds

| Level | New Buildings | Existing Buildings |
|-------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Certified** | 30 – 35 pts | 25 – 29 pts |
| **Silver** | 36 – 41 pts | 30 – 34 pts |
| **Gold** | 42 – 50 pts | 35 – 42 pts |
| **Platinum** | 51 pts and above | 43 pts and above |

## IGBC SB 2020 vs. GRIHA — Key Differences

| Aspect | IGBC SB 2020 | GRIHA (V6/V2019) |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| Rating type | Certified/Silver/Gold/Platinum | 1–5 Stars |
| Building types | New AND Existing separately scored | New construction only |
| Energy section | 20 pts (largest) | 17–20 pts |
| Materials: Existing | 1 pt only | Same points new/existing |
| Governing body | IGBC (CII) | GRIHA Council |
| International recognition | LEED family (USGBC MOU) | UN GRIHA recognition |

## Section Descriptions

### Section 1 — Site Planning and Design (7 pts New / 7 pts Existing)
Compliance with local building regulations (Mandatory), erosion and sediment control during construction, sustainable landscape design (native plants, green cover), heat island reduction for roof and non-roof surfaces, and green education to raise occupant awareness.

**Key criteria:** Local Building Regulations (Mandatory), Erosion Control (1 pt), Sustainable Landscape (3 pts), Heat Island Reduction (2 pts), Green Education (1 pt)

### Section 2 — Water Conservation (8 pts New / 7 pts Existing)
Both rainwater harvesting and water-efficient plumbing fixtures are mandatory prerequisites. Additional points for the extent of rainwater harvesting system, efficiency level of fixtures, and installation of water meters.

**Key criteria:** Rainwater Harvesting Roof (Mandatory), Water Efficient Plumbing Fixtures (Mandatory), Rainwater Harvesting Credit (4/3 pts), Water Efficient Fixtures Credit (3/3 pts), Water Metering (1/1 pt)

### Section 3 — Energy Efficiency (20 pts New / 20 pts Existing)
Largest section. Minimum energy performance is mandatory. Credits for passive architecture strategies, enhanced energy efficiency (above mandatory minimum), on-site renewable energy, energy-efficient appliances, and energy metering.

**Key criteria:** Minimum Energy Efficiency (Mandatory), Passive Architecture (3 pts), Enhanced Energy Efficiency (10 pts), On-site Renewable Energy (5 pts), Energy Saving Appliances (1 pt), Energy Metering (1 pt)

### Section 4 — Building Materials and Resources (8 pts New / 1 pt Existing)
Significant difference between new and existing buildings. New buildings earn up to 8 pts through eco-labelled materials, alternative construction technologies, alternative wood-based materials, and waste management during construction. Existing buildings earn 1 pt for a formal Green Procurement Policy.

**Key criteria:** Waste Segregation (Mandatory), Green Procurement Policy (NA/1 pt), Eco-labelled Materials (3/NA pts), Alternative Construction Technologies (3/NA pts), Alternate Wood-based Materials (1/NA pt), Construction Waste Handling (1/NA pt)

### Section 5 — Health and Well-being (7 pts New / 5 pts Existing)
Fresh air ventilation is mandatory. Credits for daylighting, using low-emitting materials (new buildings only), eco-friendly cleaning chemicals, access to safe drinking water, eco-friendly refrigerants (new buildings only), and universal design.

**Key criteria:** Fresh Air Ventilation (Mandatory), No Smoking (Mandatory), Daylighting (2 pts), Low-emitting Materials (1/NA pt), Housekeeping Chemicals (1 pt), Drinking Water (1 pt), Refrigerants (1/NA pt), Universal Design (1 pt)

### Section 6 — Green Measures Beyond the Fence (4 pts New / 4 pts Existing)
Credits for implementing sustainability initiatives in the surrounding neighbourhood or community — going beyond the building's own site boundary.

**Key criteria:** Green Measures Beyond the Fence (4 pts)

### Section 7 — Innovation and Performance (6 pts New / 6 pts Existing)
Innovation in the design process (up to 4 pts), demonstrated energy and water performance improvement (1 pt for existing buildings), green measures cost analysis (1 pt for new buildings), and involvement of an IGBC Accredited Professional (1 pt).

**Key criteria:** Innovation in Design Process (4 pts), Water and Energy Performance (NA/1 pt), Green Measures Cost Analysis (1/NA pt), IGBC Accredited Professional (1 pt)

## Mandatory Prerequisites

Before any points can be claimed, projects must comply with all Mandatory Requirements:

1. **SPD MR 1** — Local Building Regulations (site section)
2. **WC MR 1** — Rainwater Harvesting, Roof (must install at minimum scale)
3. **WC MR 2** — Water Efficient Plumbing Fixtures (minimum flow rates)
4. **EE MR 1** — Minimum Energy Efficiency (ECBC/NBC compliance)
5. **BMR MR 1** — Segregation of Post-occupancy Waste (wet/dry/hazardous)
6. **HWB MR 1** — Minimum Fresh Air Ventilation (ASHRAE 62.1 or NBC)
7. **HWB MR 2** — No Smoking Premises (complete no-smoking policy)

Failure to meet any mandatory prerequisite disqualifies the project from certification.
