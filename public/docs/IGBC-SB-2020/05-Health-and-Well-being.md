## HWB MR 1 — Minimum Fresh Air Ventilation (Mandatory)

All occupied spaces must receive minimum fresh air per ASHRAE Standard 62.1 or National Building Code.

**Minimum Requirements:**
- ≥ 10 L/s/person fresh air in all air-conditioned occupied spaces
- Natural ventilation systems must provide ≥ 3 air changes per hour

No points — gate requirement. Cannot claim any HWB credits without this.

---

## HWB MR 2 — No Smoking Premises (Mandatory)

A complete no-smoking policy must be in place covering all enclosed areas of the building.

**Requirements:**
- Signage posted at all entrances and throughout the building
- Written no-smoking policy document
- Designated outdoor smoking area (if provided): ≥ 7.5 m from all entrances, air intakes, and operable windows
- Compliance enforced and monitored

No points — mandatory requirement.

## HWB Credit 1 — Daylighting (2 pts New / 2 pts Existing)

### Intent
Provide natural daylighting to reduce lighting energy use and improve occupant health and productivity.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Daylight factor ≥ 2% in ≥ 50% of occupied floor area | 1 |
| Daylight factor ≥ 2% in ≥ 75% of occupied floor area | 2 |
| **Total** | **2** |

**Daylight factor** = (Interior illuminance / Exterior illuminance) × 100%
Target: DF ≥ 2% ensures adequate daylight for most visual tasks without supplemental lighting during daytime.

**Daylight Design Strategies:**
- Larger windows with external shading to prevent glare
- Light shelves to bounce daylight deep into floor plates
- Interior partitions with glazed panels to allow daylight penetration
- White or light-coloured interior walls (reflectance ≥ 0.70)
- Skylights or roof monitors for deep floor plates

**Simulation Tools:** DIALux, Radiance, IES-VE, VELUX Daylight Visualizer.

## HWB Credit 2 — Low-emitting Materials (1 pt New / N/A Existing)

### Intent
To reduce indoor air pollutants from paints, adhesives, and finishes that off-gas Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs).

**Requirements for 1 point (New Buildings only):**

All of the following must be met:

| Product Category | VOC Limit |
|-----------------|-----------|
| Interior paints (flat/matte) | ≤ 50 g/L |
| Interior paints (non-flat) | ≤ 150 g/L |
| Anti-rust primers | ≤ 250 g/L |
| Adhesives (general) | ≤ 50 g/L |
| Sealants (architectural) | ≤ 250 g/L |
| Carpet adhesives | ≤ 50 g/L |

## HWB Credit 3 — Eco-friendly Housekeeping Chemicals (1 pt New / 1 pt Existing)

**Requirements for 1 point:**
- ≥ 80% of cleaning products used in the building are certified eco-friendly:
  - Green Seal (GS-37, GS-40) certified
  - Ecolabel certified
  - Biodegradable and phosphate-free
  - pH neutral or near-neutral (6–8)
- Cleaning policy and procurement records maintained

Eco-friendly cleaning avoids harmful phosphates (cause algal bloom), bleach-heavy formulas (toxic effluent), and aerosol propellants.

## HWB Credit 4 — Access to Quality Drinking Water (1 pt New / 1 pt Existing)

**Requirements for 1 point:**
- Drinking water must meet IS 10500:2012 quality standards
- NABL-accredited laboratory water quality test report (tested within last 6 months)
- Drinking water dispenser or water cooler accessible within 50 m of all occupied areas
- Annual testing programme established

**IS 10500:2012 Key Parameters:**
- Total dissolved solids (TDS): ≤ 500 mg/L (acceptable), ≤ 2000 mg/L (permissible)
- pH: 6.5 – 8.5
- E. coli: NIL in 100 mL
- Turbidity: ≤ 1 NTU

## HWB Credit 5 — Eco-friendly Refrigerants (1 pt New / N/A Existing)

**Requirements for 1 point (New Buildings only):**
All HVAC refrigerants must have:
- ODP (Ozone Depletion Potential) = 0
- GWP (Global Warming Potential) ≤ 150

**Qualifying Refrigerants:**

| Refrigerant | ODP | GWP | Notes |
|------------|-----|-----|-------|
| R-717 (Ammonia) | 0 | 0 | Natural refrigerant; safety precautions required |
| R-290 (Propane) | 0 | 3 | Natural; flammable; small systems only |
| R-744 (CO₂) | 0 | 1 | Transcritical systems; high pressure |
| R-1234yf | 0 | 4 | HFO; low GWP |
| R-1234ze | 0 | 7 | HFO; suitable for chillers |

**Does not qualify:** R-410A (GWP = 2088), R-32 (GWP = 675) — these are zero-ODP but high-GWP.

## HWB Credit 6 — Universal Design (1 pt New / 1 pt Existing)

**Requirements for 1 point:**
- Building is designed/retrofitted to be accessible for persons with disabilities and elderly users
- Compliance with:
  - Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act 2016 (RPWD Act)
  - Harmonised Guidelines and Space Standards for Barrier-Free Built Environment 2021 (MoHUA)

**Key Accessible Features:**

| Feature | Requirement |
|---------|------------|
| Main entrance ramp | Slope ≤ 1:12 (8.3%); width ≥ 1.5 m; handrails both sides |
| Doorways | Clear opening ≥ 900 mm |
| Corridors | Clear width ≥ 1500 mm |
| Accessible toilets | At least 1 on each floor; 1800 × 1750 mm minimum; grab bars |
| Lift | Minimum 1100 × 1400 mm car; braille panel; audio announcements |
| Parking | ≥ 2 accessible bays (4800 mm wide); ISA symbol; nearest to entrance |
| Tactile paving | Warning strips at step edges, hazards; directional strips at entrances |
