## EE MR 1 — Minimum Energy Efficiency (Mandatory)

**Required for all buildings:**
- New buildings: Must comply with ECBC (Energy Conservation Building Code) 2017 or state ECBC
- Existing buildings: Must conduct an energy audit per BEE guidelines; implement no-cost/low-cost recommendations

Without this, no energy credits can be claimed.

## EE Credit 1 — Passive Architecture (3 pts New / 3 pts Existing)

### Intent
To reduce energy demand through climate-responsive building design — maximising natural light and ventilation while minimising solar heat gain.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Building orientation optimised: long axis within 20° of East-West | 1 |
| External shading devices on all East/West/South facades | 1 |
| High-performance glazing: SHGC ≤ 0.25 and U-value ≤ 2.0 W/m²K | 1 |
| **Total** | **3** |

### Climate-Specific Passive Design

| Climate Zone | Priority Strategy |
|-------------|------------------|
| **Composite** (Delhi, Agra, Jaipur) | Shading + insulation + moderate WWR |
| **Hot and Dry** (Jodhpur, Bikaner) | Small windows + thick walls + courtyards |
| **Warm and Humid** (Mumbai, Kochi, Chennai) | Cross-ventilation + shading + low thermal mass |
| **Moderate** (Bengaluru, Pune) | Balanced glazing + mild shading |
| **Cold** (Shimla, Leh) | South-facing glazing + heavy insulation + double glazing |

## EE Credit 2 — Enhanced Energy Efficiency (10 pts New / 10 pts Existing)

### Intent
To achieve energy performance significantly better than the ECBC/NBC baseline through efficient HVAC, lighting, and building envelope design.

### Points Scale (% Improvement over ECBC Baseline)

| Improvement | Points (New) | Points (Existing) |
|------------|-------------|------------------|
| 10% | 1 | 1 |
| 15% | 2 | 2 |
| 20% | 4 | 4 |
| 25% | 5 | 5 |
| 30% | 7 | 7 |
| 35% | 8 | 8 |
| 40% | 9 | 9 |
| 45% or more | 10 | 10 |

### Key Efficiency Measures

**HVAC:**
- Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) systems: seasonal EER typically 3.5–5.0
- Chilled water systems with variable speed drives (VSD) on pumps and fans
- Chiller COP ≥ 5.5 for large buildings (>500 TR)
- Economiser (free cooling) cycles when ambient conditions permit

**Lighting:**
- LED luminaires throughout: LPD ≤ 5 W/m² for offices
- Daylight-linked controls (photosensors) in perimeter zones
- Occupancy sensors in meeting rooms, toilets, storerooms
- Task lighting at workstations instead of general lighting

**Envelope:**
- Roof: U-value ≤ 0.4 W/m²K (insulated)
- Opaque walls: U-value ≤ 0.44 W/m²K
- Glazing: U-value ≤ 2.0, SHGC ≤ 0.25 for air-conditioned spaces

**Documentation:** Energy simulation report, equipment schedules, BEE star certificates.

## EE Credit 3 — On-site Renewable Energy (5 pts New / 5 pts Existing)

### Points Scale (% of Annual Energy Demand from On-site Renewables)

| Renewable % | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| 1% | 1 |
| 5% | 2 |
| 10% | 3 |
| 15% | 4 |
| 20% or more | 5 |

### Eligible Technologies
- **Solar PV:** Rooftop or carport; 1 kWp ≈ 1400–1600 units/year
- **Solar Thermal:** For domestic hot water; SWH replaces electric geysers
- **Wind (micro):** For buildings in coastal or high-wind locations
- **Biogas:** From on-site organic waste → used for cooking gas or power

### Solar PV Sizing Reference

| Roof Area Available | Approximate kWp | Annual Generation |
|-------------------|----------------|------------------|
| 100 m² | ~10–15 kWp | 14,000–24,000 units |
| 500 m² | ~50–75 kWp | 70,000–120,000 units |
| 1,000 m² | ~100–150 kWp | 1,40,000–2,40,000 units |

**Documentation:** PV system design, generation calculations, net metering agreement.

## EE Credit 4 — Energy Saving Appliances (1 pt New / 1 pt Existing)

**Requirements for 1 point:**
- ≥ 80% of installed appliances in the building are BEE 4-star or 5-star rated
- Applies to: refrigerators, washing machines, dishwashers, water coolers, vending machines, computers, printers

Alternatively: All appliances certified under the ENERGY STAR programme (for imported equipment).

**Documentation:** Schedule of appliances with BEE star ratings.

---

## EE Credit 5 — Energy Metering (1 pt New / 1 pt Existing)

**Requirements for 1 point:**
- Electricity sub-meters installed for each major end-use:
  - HVAC (chiller plant, AHUs separately)
  - Lighting (floor-wise or zone-wise)
  - Plug loads / power sockets
  - Common areas (lobby, corridors, basement)
  - Renewable energy generation (separate generation meter)
- Meter data logged and monthly energy reports generated
- Energy dashboard or Building Management System (BMS) accessible to facility team

**Documentation:** Electrical single-line diagram showing sub-meter locations, sample BMS screenshot/report.
