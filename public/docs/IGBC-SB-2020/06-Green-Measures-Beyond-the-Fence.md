## GM Credit 1 — Green Measures Beyond the Fence (4 pts New / 4 pts Existing)

### Intent
To extend the positive sustainability impact of the building beyond its own site boundary, benefiting the surrounding neighbourhood, community, or ecosystem.

### Points Scale

Each qualifying measure earns 1 point. Maximum 4 points from any combination of qualifying measures.

---

### Qualifying Measures (1 pt each, max 4 pts)

#### 1. Energy — Green Power for Neighbours
- Supply surplus renewable energy to neighbouring buildings or the local grid (net metering or direct PPA)
- OR participate in a community solar programme benefiting local households

#### 2. Water — Public Rainwater Harvesting
- Install a rainwater harvesting system that also benefits public areas or adjacent properties
- OR provide treated STP effluent for public landscape irrigation (parks, roads)

#### 3. Mobility — Green Transportation
- Provide electric vehicle (EV) charging stations accessible to the public (minimum 2 points)
- OR operate a company shuttle/carpooling programme reducing private car trips by ≥ 20%
- OR provide safe, covered bicycle parking and changing facilities for public use

#### 4. Biodiversity — Community Green Space
- Create or maintain publicly accessible garden, park, or water body on or adjacent to the site
- Area ≥ 500 m² with native planting, open 24 hours
- OR adopt a stretch of public road/median for greening (minimum 100 m length)

#### 5. Waste — Community Waste Management
- Set up a community-level waste segregation and recycling centre serving residents within 500 m
- OR provide composting facility that also accepts organic waste from adjacent properties/residents

#### 6. Education — Green Building Awareness
- Open the building for regular public tours showcasing green features (at least monthly)
- OR run a structured green building awareness programme for local schools (minimum 4 sessions/year)
- OR publish open educational content (online or print) about the building's sustainability features

#### 7. Health — Community Wellness
- Provide access to sports/fitness facilities to neighbourhood residents during off-peak hours
- OR establish health/wellness programmes (yoga, medical camps) open to surrounding community

#### 8. Local Economy — Green Jobs and Procurement
- Employ ≥ 50% of service staff (security, housekeeping, maintenance) from within 5 km of the building
- OR source ≥ 30% of goods and services from local businesses (within 50 km)

---

### Documentation Required
- For each measure claimed: description, evidence of implementation, and impact (number of beneficiaries, quantity of energy/water/waste handled)
- For public-access measures: visitor count or community testimonials
- For education programmes: attendance records, session schedules
