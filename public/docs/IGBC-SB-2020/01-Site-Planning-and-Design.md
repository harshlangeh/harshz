## SPD MR 1 — Local Building Regulations (Mandatory)

### Intent
Ensure the building complies with all applicable local urban planning, zoning, setback, FAR (Floor Area Ratio), and ground coverage regulations.

**What's Required:**
- Building plan approval from local authority (BDA, MCGM, HMDA, etc.)
- Occupancy certificate or building permit on record
- No violations of setback, height, or coverage regulations

**Documentation:** Approved building plan, commencement certificate, statutory approvals.

---

## SPD Credit 1 — Erosion and Sedimentation Control (1 pt New / 1 pt Existing)

### Intent
To prevent soil erosion and sediment-laden runoff from leaving the construction site and polluting adjacent water bodies, roads, and drainage systems.

**Requirements for 1 point:**
- Erosion and Sedimentation Control (ESC) plan developed before work begins
- Silt fences or straw wattles installed at site perimeter and downhill drainage paths
- Check dams or sediment basins at storm drain inlets
- Site inspected weekly during rain events; damaged controls repaired within 24 hours

**Documentation:** ESC plan, site photographs, weekly inspection logs.

## SPD Credit 2 — Sustainable Landscape (3 pts New / 3 pts Existing)

### Intent
To promote ecological landscaping that minimises water demand, supports local biodiversity, and increases green cover on and around the building site.

| Sub-criterion | Points New | Points Existing |
|---------------|-----------|----------------|
| ≥ 20% of total site area as vegetated/green cover | 1 | 1 |
| ≥ 50% of all planted species are native or adaptive (no permanent irrigation) | 1 | 1 |
| No invasive species used; existing mature trees retained where possible | 1 | 1 |
| **Total** | **3** | **3** |

**Native vs. Adaptive vs. Invasive:**
- **Native:** Indigenous to the local bioregion (e.g., Neem, Peepal, Gulmohar in North India)
- **Adaptive:** Established, non-invasive species that thrive without supplemental watering
- **Invasive:** Species that spread aggressively and harm local ecology (e.g., Lantana, Parthenium) — must not be planted

**Documentation:** Landscape plan with species list, annotated with native/adaptive/invasive status.

## SPD Credit 3 — Heat Island Reduction (2 pts New / 2 pts Existing)

### Intent
To reduce the Urban Heat Island Effect through use of high-reflectance roof and paving materials.

| Sub-criterion | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| **Non-roof:** ≥ 50% of non-roof hard paved areas have SRI ≥ 29, OR ≥ 50% of parking is shaded | 1 |
| **Roof:** ≥ 50% of roof area has SRI ≥ 78 (cool roof) | 1 |
| **Total** | **2** |

**SRI Reference Values:**

| Material | Typical SRI |
|---------|------------|
| White lime/cement wash | 85 – 95 |
| China mosaic tile (white) | 70 – 85 |
| Light-grey concrete | 35 – 50 |
| Dark bitumen / asphalt | 0 – 10 |
| Bare RCC (unpainted) | 20 – 35 |

**Documentation:** Roof plan with material SRI values, manufacturer test certificates.

## SPD Credit 4 — Green Education (1 pt New / 1 pt Existing)

### Intent
To raise environmental awareness among building occupants and visitors through education about the building's green features.

**Requirements for 1 point:**
- Install interpretive signage explaining at least 3 major green features (solar panels, rainwater system, STP, energy-efficient HVAC, etc.)
- OR conduct at least 1 annual green education event/workshop for occupants
- OR create and maintain a dedicated page on the building owner's website about the green features

**Documentation:** Photographs of signage, event records, website screenshot.
