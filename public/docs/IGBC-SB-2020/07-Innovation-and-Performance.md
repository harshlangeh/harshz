## IP Credit 1 — Innovation in Design Process (4 pts New / 4 pts Existing)

### Intent
To reward projects that demonstrate innovative sustainability strategies that are not covered by other IGBC SB 2020 criteria, or that achieve exemplary performance far exceeding existing credit thresholds.

### Points Available
Up to 4 points — evaluated by IGBC on a case-by-case basis. Each approved innovation earns 1 point (maximum 4 innovations).

### What Qualifies as Innovation

#### A. Exemplary Performance (exceeding maximum IGBC credit thresholds)

| Credit | Normal Max | Exemplary Threshold |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| Rainwater harvesting | 4 months demand covered | Covers 6+ months |
| Water efficiency | 50% reduction | 75%+ reduction |
| Enhanced energy efficiency | 45% over baseline | 60%+ over baseline |
| Renewable energy | 20% of demand | 40%+ of demand |
| Green measures beyond fence | 4 measures | 6+ measures |

#### B. New Technologies Not in IGBC SB 2020
- Net-Zero Energy Building (NZEB): generation = consumption annually
- Net-Zero Water Building: 100% water self-sufficiency
- Living Building Challenge alignment
- WELL Building Standard certification (health focus)
- Passive House certification
- Building Integrated Photovoltaics (BIPV) at significant scale
- Smart building with AI/ML energy optimisation
- Green roofs or vertical gardens at scale with measurable impact

#### C. Unique Green Processes
- Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) demonstrating carbon-neutral embodied carbon
- Carbon offset programme registered with a recognised standard (Gold Standard, Verra VCS)
- Construction with zero waste to landfill (100% C&D waste diverted)

### How to Submit
Prepare a 2–4 page innovation brief per claim:
1. Summary of innovation
2. Why it's not captured in existing IGBC SB 2020 criteria
3. Quantified environmental benefit
4. Third-party verification (if available)

## IP Credit 2 — Water and Energy Performance (N/A for New / 1 pt for Existing)

**Only for Existing Buildings.**

### Intent
For existing buildings undergoing IGBC SB 2020 assessment, demonstrate measurable improvement in water and energy performance through operational data.

**Requirements for 1 point:**
Submit 24 months of consecutive energy and water consumption data showing:
- ≥ 10% reduction in Energy Use Intensity (EUI: kWh/m²/year) vs. the building's own baseline (previous 2 years before green measures implemented)
- ≥ 10% reduction in Water Use Intensity (WUI: L/person/day or m³/m²/year) over the same period

**Documentation:** BMS or utility bill data (monthly for 24 months), calculation of EUI and WUI before and after.

## IP Credit 3 — Green Measures Cost Analysis (1 pt for New / N/A for Existing)

**Only for New Buildings.**

### Intent
To encourage financial analysis of green investments, helping clients understand the payback and long-term value of sustainable building features.

**Requirements for 1 point:**
A Life Cycle Cost Analysis (LCCA) must be conducted covering:
- Capital cost premium of all green measures over a conventional baseline
- Annual savings in energy, water, and maintenance
- Payback period for each major green measure
- Net Present Value (NPV) of green measures over a 25-year period
- Discount rate and escalation rates clearly stated

**Documentation:** LCCA report with all assumptions, cashflow table, payback summary.

## IP Credit 4 — IGBC Accredited Professional (1 pt New / 1 pt Existing)

### Intent
To ensure projects are guided by a qualified professional with formal training in IGBC rating systems, improving documentation quality and compliance.

**Requirements for 1 point:**
At least one member of the core project team (architect, engineer, or sustainability consultant) must hold a current IGBC Accredited Professional (IGBC AP) credential.

### IGBC AP Examination
- Administered by CII IGBC
- Covers all IGBC rating systems (Green Homes, Green Buildings, LEED India, SB 2020)
- Credential must be current (not expired) at the time of IGBC SB 2020 submission
- IGBC AP must be actively involved in the project (not just a token credential)

**Documentation:** IGBC AP credential certificate, signed declaration of involvement in the project.
